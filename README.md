##  The Vision
Ritah Styles is more than a fashion house; it is an atelier dedicated to the art of the perfect fit. Established in 2022, we blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary feminine aesthetics. This digital space serves as our portfolio, showcasing a world of **Warm Cream, Rose Gold, and Botanical elegance.**

##  Artistry & Services
* **The Ivory Grace:** Bespoke bridal wear designed for your most unforgettable moments.
* **Midnight Bloom:** High-end evening gowns that command the room.
* **The Atelier Experience:** A meticulous made-to-measure process, ensuring every garment feels like a second skin.
* **Restyling & Alterations:** Expertly breathing new life into cherished pieces with precision and care.

---

##  Technical Craftsmanship
The website is engineered with the same attention to detail as our garments:
* **Elegant UI:** Built using a sophisticated palette of `#faf7f4` (Cream) and `#c49a8a` (Rose Gold).
* **Fluid Responsive Design:** A custom CSS architecture that looks flawless from mobile screens to desktop monitors.
* **Luxury Typography:** Featuring *Cormorant Garamond* for timeless headings and *Dancing Script* for a personal, handwritten touch.

---

##  Project Structure
* `index.html` — The structural foundation of the boutique experience.
* `styles.css` — The "fabric" of the site, containing all visual tokens and animations.
* `RITAH.jpg` — The central brand identity asset.
