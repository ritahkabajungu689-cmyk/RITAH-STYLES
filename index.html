How We Work

Our Craft

Every great garment begins with a conversation and ends with a smile. Here is our time-honoured process.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    We sit down with you over tea, learn about your vision, your occasion, and what makes you feel most beautiful. This is where the magic begins.

  2. 02

    Design & Fabric

    We sketch your garment and guide you through our curated fabric collection — silks, satins, lace, and linens sourced from the finest mills.

  3. 03

    Cut & Stitch

    Your pattern is cut by hand and stitched with care. Each seam is considered, every detail deliberate. We enjoy the process as much as the result.

  4. 04

    Fitting & Finish

    We bring you in for fittings, refining every curve and hem until the garment is absolutely perfect. We won't let you leave until you feel like the best version of yourself.
